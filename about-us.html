About Us

We’re working to safeguard wellbeing across generations in Australia and around the world.

At MSDI, we’re passionate about creating a better world, and we’re committed to finding sustainable solutions to global challenges. We do this through research, education and engagement, and bring a diverse range of capabilities and skills to our mission.

But we know we can’t do it alone.

The need for transformational change has never been more urgent, with little time left for us to secure a safe future. That means that now, more than ever, collaboration and partnerships are the key to success. MSDI is proud to be part of Monash University — Australia’s largest university — and to bring together the strength of Monash in impact-focused partnerships with industry, government and community.

With over 150 staff and PhD students, we combine rigour with relevance, establish transdisciplinary teams, co-produce knowledge and identify pathways and innovative solutions to achieve the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development. We measure our impact by our ability to apply research to practical problems and to influence change in policies and practice. And our award-winning interdisciplinary education programs build capacity and train sustainable development leaders of the future.

Carefully nurtured partnerships underpin everything we do. We’re currently working with over 150 local, regional and global partners on some of the biggest challenges of our times. In 2020, we want to increase our impact in what must be a transformational decade.
